POC Activity

MIDA have collaborated with Orgenesis Inc, (NASDAQ: ORGS), a leading global biotech company focused on cell and gene therapies, to establish point of care (“POC”) centers at hospitals and other medical institutions across western Europe.

The POC centers are processing facilities dedicated to cell and gene therapies placed in medical/research institutions with the goal significant enhance the accessibility to advanced cell and gene therapies by lowering the processing and delivery costs and eliminating the logistical and administrative challenges related to centralized production of such therapies.

Develop, optimize and manufacture cell and gene therapies

Orgenesis and MIDA plan to deploy Orgenesis Mobile Processing Units and Labs (“OMPULs”) at leading hospitals for the onsite development of promising cell and gene therapies and immunotherapies from MIDA. The OMPULs are multi-purpose, mobile, autonomous good manufacturing practice (GMP) facilities used to develop, optimize, and manufacture cell and gene therapies at the point of care.

Via the collaboration, the teams will align with various hospitals with projects focused on scaling the therapies through to commercialization with regulatory compliance and governmental approval standards as guiding principles.
