IPS Innovation Lab

In MIDA Biotech B.V. we believe in “game changing” stem cell therapies. Our mission is to create an innovation platform that brings together scientists, engineers, and technologies, to make fundamental innovations in stem cell therapies. Our model is Bell’s and Edison’s labs where some of the most influential innovations in human technology were invented.

Stem cell manufacturing

The manufacturing of stem cells for clinical applications is incredibly expensive. Production of induced pluripotent stem (iPS) cells at reasonable cost for every person will transform medicine. Imagine how tissue replacements for incurable diseases such as blindness, diabetes, Parkinson’s disease could transform the lives of patients worldwide.

In MIDA Biotech B.V. we aim to create technologies for manufacturing of autologous iPS cells that will make it economically feasible to produce any graft.

Neurodegenerative disease

Diseases of the peripheral nervous system are devastating. Sensory nerves are damaged by many chemotherapies, and limit cures of cancers.

Motor nerves are damaged in amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), leading to paralysis and death. In MIDA Biotech B.V. we licenced and support the development of technologies that could promote cures of peripheral nerves in disease and injury.

Our Location

Our stem-cell platform Laboratory is located in Leiden Bioscience Park (LBSP), the largest Life Sciences and Health cluster in the Netherlands and one of Europe´s leading innovation hubs. Home to over 152 companies LBP fosters a collaborative ecosystem of innovative companies, research institutes and academia.

LBSP´s environment provides MIDA with great access to human talent, innovation culture and scientific knowledge.
