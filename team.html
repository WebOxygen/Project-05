The Company

MIDA Biotech is a leading biotech company in the field of gene and stem cell therapies. Our focus is on research and development in the field of stem cells and their application in anti-aging, biobanking, and regenerative medicine.

MIDA is a center of excellence and supports other biotech companies through its innovative concepts.

Dr. Shiri Littner Zeltzer

Managing director

Dr Shiri Littner Zeltzer joined MIDA in 2020 as a Managing Director.

Dr Littner Zeltzer attained her DVM (Doctor of veterinary Medicine) in the Koret school of Veterinary Medicine of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem and a BSc in Biology sciences in the faculty of science of Tel Aviv University, In Israel.

Prior to joining MIDA, Dr. Littner Zeltzer have fulfilled several positions in Medivet, one of the Largest Veterinary clinics group in the UK. She worked as the head of customer success in UENI, a growing global startup company with an aim to support small businesses in creating digital presence.

Prof. Ph.D. Micha Drukker

Senior advisor

Prof. Ph.D. Micha Drukker

Senior advisor

Prof. Micha Drukker is an expert in stem cell research for clinical applications. He graduated from the Hebrew University and was a postdoctoral scholar at Stanford University Medical School in the lab of Prof. Irving Weissman.

He became and received tenure as a group leader and head of the induced pluripotent stem (iPS) cell core facility at the Helmholtz Center in Munich in 2012 and 2018. Micha became a full professor in 2020 in Leiden University within the Leiden Academic Center for Drug Research (LACDR).

Micha’s expertise lies in the applications of stem cells as “living drugs” in novel human therapeutics. His lab is leading the manufacturing (GMP) of clinical-grade human PS cells from donors that are immunologically matched to approximately 100 million individuals in Europe without immune rejection or suppression. His research profile combines stem cell biology, disease modeling in the dish using iPS cells, biochemistry, and immunology.

His lab made pioneering discoveries including mechanisms of stem cell immunogenicity, the cell progenitors that give rise to vast tissues in the human body for example the heart, how mutations lead to congenital defects of the nervous system, and his lab developed technologies for example for iPS cell genomics. Micha was awarded prestigious grants including by the German Research Foundation (DFG), Bundesministerium für Bildung und Forschung (BMBF), Horizon 2020, Volkswagen Foundation, the AЯRE Foundation and more, and his publication profile include top caliber journals including Nature Biotechnology, Molecular Cell, PNAS and more.

Our Location

Our stem-cell platform Laboratory is located in Leiden Bioscience Park (LBSP), the largest Life Sciences and Health cluster in the Netherlands and one of Europe´s leading innovation hubs. Home to over 152 companies LBP fosters a collaborative ecosystem of innovative companies, research institutes and academia.

LBSP´s environment provides MIDA with great access to human talent, innovation culture and scientific knowledge.
